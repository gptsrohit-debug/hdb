💌 Dil Ki Awaz — Shayari 💌

✦ Shayari I ✦
Arushi, tera naam hi meri dhadkan ban gaya hai,
Tera har khayal meri rooh ka armaan ban gaya hai,
Bas ek mahina aur, phir tera woh khaas din aayega,
Mere liye toh har din hi tera janamdin ban gaya hai.
— 💕 —
Chand bhi sharma jaaye teri khubsurti dekh kar,
Sitare bhi jhuk jaaye teri ek muskaan par,
Us din dua hai meri, har khushi tere kadmon mein ho,
Aur main rahun hamesha tere dil ke paas har pal.
✦ Shayari II ✦
Teri aankhon mein ek duniya hai alag,
Jahan khwaishein hain aur khaamoshiyaan bhi,
Teri muskaan pe aasmaan bhi ruk jaata hai,
Aur dil ko chain aata hai tujhse mil ke bhi.
— 🌹 —
Tu hai to bahar hai, tu hai to roshni hai,
Teri ek chhoti si ada dil churaa leti hai,
Janamdin pe yahi dua hai tere liye, Arushi,
Khushiyon ki baarish har roz tujhe nahlaa leti rahe.
✦ Shayari III ✦
Ek mahine baad jo din aayega,
Woh din mere liye bhi khaas hoga,
Kyunki teri khushi meri khushi hai,
Tera janam hi mera tyohaar hoga.
— 💫 —
Phool bhi sharma jaaye tere aage,
Chandni bhi jhuk jaaye teri raah mein,
Tera yeh janamdin roshni bane,
Aur pyaar barse teri chaah mein.

💌 Ek Khat — Sirf Tere Liye 💌

26 April 2026  |  Dilli, Hindustaan
Pyaari Arushi,

Kaafi waqt se dil mein ek baat thi — aaj likh raha hoon kyunki alfaaz se hi samjhaa sakta hoon jo aankhein nahi keh paati. Tera naam sunne se hi kuch alag feel hota hai, jaise subah ki pehli roshni, jaise barishon mein mitti ki khushboo — bilkul sahi, bilkul apna.

Teri aankhon mein woh sitare hain
jo raat ko bhi subah bana dete hain,
Tere haathon ki chhoti si chuaan se
dil ke sare dard mit jaate hain.

Tu jab muskurati hai, Arushi,
toh lagta hai duniya ruk gayi hai —
Sirf main hoon, sirf tu hai,
aur yeh pal hamesha ke liye ruk gayi hai.

Ek mahine baad tera woh khaas din aayega — 27 May — aur main soch raha hoon ki kya kya karunga. Shayad phool laaunga, shayad ek chota sa cake, par sabse pehle yeh khat — sirf teri khatir likha hua.

Tujhe pata hai na, teri ek muskaan pe main kitna kuch bhool jaata hoon? Sab gusse, sab chintaayein — sab gaayab. Tu magical hai, Arushi. Aur main khush hoon ki yeh jadoo mujhpar hai.

Chand ko teri zaroorat hai roshni ke liye,
Phoolon ko teri zaroorat hai khushboo ke liye,
Mujhe teri zaroorat hai — bas jeene ke liye.

Teri hansi meri taaqat hai,
Teri yaad meri raat ka sahara hai,
Tu duur ho toh bhi paas lagti hai —
kyunki tu mere dil mein zyada rehti hai.

Is janamdin pe sirf ek dua hai meri — teri zindagi mein utni khushiyaan ho jitni taarein hain raat ke aasmaaan mein. Tera har sapna pura ho, har dua qabool ho, aur jo bhi chahti ho — woh tujhe mil jaaye.

Kyunki tu deserve karti hai duniya ki saari khushiyaan.
Aur thodi si extra bhi. 💕

Happy Birthday, meri jaan —
27 May ko aasmaan bhi muskurayega
kyunki us din tu paida hui thi,
aur yeh duniya thodi aur khoobsurat ho gayi thi.

Teri dhadkan mein meri dua hai,
Teri muskaan mein mera jahaan hai.
Hamesha tera,   💕
